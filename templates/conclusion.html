Conclusions

The score between Biden and Trump is 5-5 in the top 10 states with highest black/afro rate.

Conclusion: No significant correlation

The score between Biden and Trump is 3-7 in the top 10 states with highest poverty rate.

Conclusion: Trump seems to be more popular in states with a higher poverty.

The score between Biden and Trump is 7-3 in the top 10 states with highest female adult rate.

Conclusion: Biden seems to be more popular in states with a higher female adult rate.

There are only 6 states with a higher male adult rate and Trump wins in 5 out of the 6 states.

Conclusion: Trump seems to be more popular in states with a higher male adult rate.
